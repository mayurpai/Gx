1. List Out Top 5 UI Web Applications & Justify, Why Do You Think They Are Good?

A. Brave Browser:
-> Invasive Ads Blocked
-> Cross-site Trackers Blocked
-> Cookies Blocked
-> Malware & Phishing Attacks Protection
-> Anonymized Network Routing

B. Medium:
-> An Online Reading And Publishing Platform
-> Minimal Use Of Color, Generous Line Spacing, Well Choosen Combination Of Typography
-> An Amazing And Effortless Editorial Experience
->  Presented With An Almost Empty White Page, Removing Clutter And Simply Providing A Clean Space To Focus On Writing.

C. Airbnb:
-> Booking A Place To Stay
-> Just Choose A Destination That Users Would Like To Go, Along With The Duration Of Their Stay And The Number Of Guests.
-> Airbnb Takes Pride In Bringing People Together And Gives The Conversation Importance Over The Transaction
-> Used Engaging Photos And Videos To Ensure That The Interface Conveys An Emotional Tone That Helps Create A Sense Of Trust Between Strangers.

D. Dropbox: 
-> One Of The Most Easily Understandable Interfaces 
-> Having A Folder And File Organizational Structure Is Easily Recognizable
-> Dropbox’s Friendly Personality, Created By Lighthearted Illustrations, Helps The User Feel Comfortable When Using The Product.
-> There’s So Much Attention To Detail, With Sequenced Color Palettes And Animated Icons, And Wonderful Illustration That Is Coordinated With All The Other Wonderful Illustrations

E. MakeMyTrip:
-> One Is Able To Book Flights, Hotels, Homestays, Holiday Packages, Trains, Buses, Cabs, Etc.
-> It Offers OneWay And Round Trip Bookings To Be Done With Clear Mentioning Beforehand On The Homepage
-> Providing Great Offers, Competitive Airfares, Exclusive Discounts, And A Seamless Online Booking Experience To Many Of Its Customers
->  Instant Notifications Ensure Current Flight Status, Instant Fare Drops, Amazing Discounts, Instant Refunds And Rebook Options, Price Comparisons And Many More Interesting Features.
-> Booking Your Flight Tickets, Hotel Stay, And Holiday Package Through Our Desktop Site Or Mobile App Can Be Done With Complete Ease And No Hassles At All
